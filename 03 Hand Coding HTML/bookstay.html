Some terms

Allomancy
Allomancy allows individuals to ingest and "burn" metals to gain various abilities, such as enhanced strength or manipulation of emotions.
Ferrochemy
involves storing attributes like health or speed in metal objects, which can later be accessed.
Hemalurgy
focuses on transferring powers from one person to another through metal spikes, often at a significant cost.
